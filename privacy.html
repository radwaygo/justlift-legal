Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Thank you for using JustLift. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All information you create (such as workout logs, exercise sets, and preferences) is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud, Apple securely stores and syncs your data across your devices using your Apple ID. We do not have access to this information.

Health Data

If you choose to connect Apple Health, the app will only read or write data that you explicitly allow. This data never leaves your device or Apple’s secure HealthKit framework. We do not collect or share any health information.

Third Parties

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external servers. Your data remains under your control and within your Apple ecosystem.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect personal data, no additional parental controls are required.

Your Rights

You may delete your app data at any time by removing the app from your device. If you use iCloud, you may also manage or delete synced data through your Apple account settings.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in this document with a new “Last updated” date.
